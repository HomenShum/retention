## What retention.sh Does

retention.sh captures agent workflows, finds cheaper validated paths, and audits those shortcuts before turning them into reusable automation.

Unlike full-crawl-every-time approaches, TA captures the trajectory once and replays the cheapest valid path. Each rerun gets cheaper as the trajectory stabilizes.
